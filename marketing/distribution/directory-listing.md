**Long description (~90 words, for the main "about" body):**
Billify is a free, no-signup invoice generator. Pick from 12 templates, choose
your currency (160 supported), add line items, tax, and a logo, then export a
polished PDF or CSV — no account, no credit card, and no server-side storage

month with 2 templates; Pro (€9/mo, or €6.58/mo billed annually) adds unlimited
invoices, all 12 templates, and logo upload. Built in is a client directory,
invoice history with paid/sent/overdue status, sequential auto-numbering, and
one-click backup/restore.
