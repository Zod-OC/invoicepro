# Listicle outreach — pitch emails + how to find targets

Ranking "best free invoice generator" listicles already have the exact buyers you
want (people searching, ready to pick a tool). Getting Billify added to 3–5 of
them is higher-intent traffic than any directory. Send 4–5 personalized emails;
expect ~1–2 to add you.

## How to find the targets
Google these (and the equivalents in your locale) and collect the top 5–10
non-competitor listicles:
- `best free invoice generator`
- `free invoice generator no signup`
- `best invoice generator 2026`
- `free invoicing software for freelancers`
- `invoice generator no account`

For each result: open it, confirm Billify ISN'T already listed, find the author
(byline → Twitter/LinkedIn/personal site/email), and note one specific thing
about their article (so the email doesn't read as a blast).

## Rules
- **Personalize the first line.** A blast that could go to anyone gets ignored.
  Reference one specific thing in their article.
- **One email per author.** If no reply in ~10 days, one polite nudge — then stop.
- **Don't claim anything untrue** ("only", "best", "most private"). The honest
  differentiators (no-signup, browser-only, 160 currencies, free no-watermark)
  are enough.
- **Make trying frictionless** — always link the `/app` editor (no signup), not
  just the homepage.
- **Track who you emailed + the outcome** so you know which listicles updated.
